Rectball

Rectball is a puzzle game for Windows, Linux, MacOS X and Android where the objective is to make the most amount of points before the time is over by tapping colour circles and making rectangles with them.

Download JAR (9 MB) Download APK (3.7 MB)

Play Store version will be released in the near future. At the moment you can run the game in Android phones by manually downloading and installing the APK. It is up to you whether to enable or not third-party sources on your phone.
